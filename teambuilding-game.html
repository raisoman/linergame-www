Linergame Teambuilding

A 1-day teambuilding exercise centered around the management of a container shipping line with a weekly Asia-Europe service. The objective is for the team to collectively maximize the earnings of the shipping line through coordination of their individual specialist roles in the game. Coordination requires teamwork both across people with similar roles in different geographies as well as across different roles. The session will be tailored to match varying skill levels across the participants.

Ideal for: Teambuilding with container shipping lines, supply chain companies, ports and terminals. Especially well suited for newly formed departments or project teams.

access_time 7 hours.
